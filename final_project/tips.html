✨Tips for dealing with Anxiety and Depression✨

  • Take a time-out. Practice yoga, listen to music, meditate, get a massage, or learn relaxation techniques. Stepping back from the problem helps clear your head.
  • Eat well-balanced meals. Do not skip any meals. keep healthful, energy-boosting snacks on hand.
  • Get enough sleep. When stressed, your body needs additional sleep and rest.
  • Count to 10 slowly. Repeat, and count to 20 if necessary.

    • ✨Tips for dealing with Bullying✨

  • Report the bullying to a trusted adult.
  • Focus on the positive.
  • Boost your confidence.
  • Be proud of who you are.
